Woosong Festival

Information about event

This year's Woosong Festival (October 11-12) will host the Woosong Song Festival and Woosong's Got Talent.
The Woosong Song Festival will be held on the first day of the Woosong Festival, Wednesday October 11.
The Woosong's Got Talent contest will be held on the second day of the Woosong Festival, Thursday October 12.
'Woosong's Got Talent' is a platform where you can show your talents and talents not only by dancing,
but also by performing, playing musical instruments, magic, and other acts.We look forward to your challenge.

Recruitment area:
Woosong Song Festival-Singing
Woosong Got Talent - Dance, performance, magic, vocal mimicry, musical instrument performance, etc

Prize money: A total of 2 million won (including prize money and prizes)
Application period: Friday, September 1, 2023, 9:00 to Friday, September 22, 2023, 18:00
How to apply: Contact the student council president of each department
Eligibility for application: Only students who have paid their student service fees
Preliminary round: Tuesday, September 26, 2023 at 18:00 (scheduled)
Preliminary round location: Woosong Arts Center (W18)
Finalist announcement: Wednesday, September 27, 2023 (scheduled)

* If there is music, the chosen song and the stage performance on the day must be the same.
